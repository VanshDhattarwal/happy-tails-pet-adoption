Frequently Asked Questions

How can I adopt a pet?

You can visit the Adopt page, choose a pet, and fill out the adoption form. Our team will contact you.

Is adoption free?

Adoption is mostly free, but small maintenance or vaccination charges may apply.

Are pets vaccinated?

Yes, all pets are fully vaccinated and checked by veterinarians before adoption.

Can I return a pet after adoption?

Yes, but only in special cases. We encourage responsible adoption.

How do I contact support?

You can use the form below or email us directly.
